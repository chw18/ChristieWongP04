Motion Graphic

portraying the atmosphere of the movie Inception

Next Project

Role
Graphic & Animation Designer

Client
Simon Fraser University

Year / Time Taken
2011 / 1 Week

Tools
Adobe Illustrator, AfterEffects

This individual project was completed in a media arts course. The aim was to create a motion graphic based on a movie, focusing on the concept of 'graphic match'.

I picked the movie Inception (2010) by Christopher Nolan as the basis for my motion graphic. I drew all the graphics in Illustrator, and then used AfterEffects for all the animation, editing, sounds, and transitional effects. As a result, I was able to effectively use the idea of graphic match to portray the atmosphere and setting of the movie Inception.
