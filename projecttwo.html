User-oriented Application

navigating within Granville Island

BlackBerry 10 Longitudinal Study

Role
Graphic Designer, Animator & Programmer

Client
Simon Fraser University

Year / Time Taken
2011 / 6 Weeks

Tools
Adobe Illustrator, Flash, Photoshop

Purpose
This team project, completed as the final project in an Information Design course, is a way-finding tablet application for a a six-year old child to navigate Granville Island.

This is a walkthrough video of our application. As our chosen user is a six-year old child, our application is tailored for this age group by using vibrant colours, simple icons, and minimal text.

My Contribution

  • Drawing a portion of the graphics using Illustrator
  • Coded the action-script in Flash
  • Designed the animation in Flash
  • Coordinated appointments with professor and teaching assistant for weekly feedback

Result
We devised a Flash prototype of this application, successfully incorporating the concepts of mapping strategies, icon representation and user-orientation learned from this course.
